# Design Notes for index.html

## Animation Style
- **"Breathing" feel**: Use smooth, gentle floating animations that feel organic and alive, like things are breathing or floating naturally through space
- **Avoid bouncy/jarring motion**: Keep animations subtle with small movements (8px or less)
- **Staggered timing**: Vary animation durations to create natural, flowing motion
- **Magic aesthetic**: Animations should feel mystical and enchanting, not mechanical

## Theme
- **Space theme**: Incorporate cosmic, celestial elements
- **Color palette**: Deep purples, blues, electric accents on dark backgrounds
- **Effects**: Animated blobs/orbs in background, glowing effects, screen blend modes
- **Overall feeling**: Futuristic, magical, ethereal

## Content Preservation
- Always preserve original content exactly as written
- Never add, remove, or alter text without explicit request
- Maintain all styling preferences and structure
